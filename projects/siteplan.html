CAFE Website Planning Document

Site Name

Cozy Corner Cafe

This name reflects the warm, inviting atmosphere of our café, where customers can relax and enjoy quality coffee and baked goods. It emphasizes comfort and a sense of community.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to showcase our café, menu offerings, location, and special events. Customers will also be able to view our hours of operation, contact details, and customer testimonials.

Scenarios

1. What are your cafe opening hour and location?

Visitors may want to know when they can visit My café and where it is located. My website should provide clear details about operating hours and a map or directions to the café.

2. Do you offer online ordering or delivery options?

Customers may be looking for convenience and want to know if they can place an order online for pickup or delivery. Your website should include information about available services, a menu, and an ordering system if applicable.

Color Schema

Primary Colors:Warm Brown (#8B4513) - Used for headings, navigation bar, and important highlights to create a cozy and inviting feel.

Secondary Colors:Soft Beige (#F5F5DC) - Used for the background and content sections to ensure readability and a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

Typography

Primary Font:Lora - Used for headings to provide an elegant, sophisticated look.

Secondary Font: Open Sans - Used for body text to ensure readability and a modern feel.
